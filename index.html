custom-elements-builder

is just a set of builders, natively scalable and designed for FRP

Builders

Based on the builder pattern, the services provided by <ceb/> are easy to discover and use. Builders work on the prototype and the behavior of a Custom Element.

Scalable

The services provided by <ceb/> are split into dedicated builders. By this way, consumer are free to use then or to build their own.

FRP

With <ceb/> , the keyword this is no more necessary. Each callbacks, get the custom element instance as their first argument.

Dependency free

<ceb/> is dependency free. Only not evergreen browsers have to be enhanced with a polyfill of the Custom Element specification.
